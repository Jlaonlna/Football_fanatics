About Football Fanatics

Our Mission

At Football Fanatics, we are passionate about bringing you the latest football news, analysis, and fan experiences. Our goal is to serve as the ultimate destination for all things football, connecting fans with their favorite teams and players.

Company Overview

Football Fanatics is a subsidiary of Fanatics, Inc., a global digital sports platform founded in 1995. We specialize in licensed sportswear, sports equipment, and merchandise for football enthusiasts worldwide.

  • CEO: Ahmed
  • Headquarters: Dubai, Uae
  • Global Presence: Over 80 offices and locations worldwide
  • Employees: More than 22,000 globally
